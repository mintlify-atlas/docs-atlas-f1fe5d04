Connect with other MyDiary users to share diary entries and stay updated with their lives.

## Finding friends

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Friends page">
    Click on the Friends section in the navigation menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse available users">
    By default, you'll see the **Profiles** tab which shows all MyDiary users who are not yet your friends.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search for specific users">
    Use the search bar at the top to find friends by:
    - Name
    - Email address
    
    The search filters results in real-time as you type.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Friend request workflow

### Sending a friend request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the user">
    Search for or browse to the user you want to add as a friend.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send the request">
    Click the **Enviar solicitud de amistad** (Send friend request) button on their profile card.
    
    The button text will change to **Cancelar solicitud** (Cancel request) to indicate your request is pending.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for acceptance">
    The other user needs to accept your request before you become friends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can cancel a pending friend request at any time by clicking the **Cancelar solicitud** button.
</Tip>

### Accepting friend requests

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Requests tab">
    Click on **Solicitudes** (Requests) in the Friends page navigation.
    
    You'll see all pending friend requests you've received.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the request">
    Each request shows:
    - The user's name
    - Their profile picture
    - Two action buttons
  </Step>

  <Step title="Accept or reject">
    - Click **Aceptar solicitud** (Accept request) to become friends
    - Click **Rechazar solicitud** (Reject request) to decline
    
    <Note>
      Accepting a request allows you both to see each other's public diary entries.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your friend list

### Viewing your friends

1. Navigate to the Friends page
2. Click the **Amigos** (Friends) tab
3. You'll see all users with whom you have an accepted friendship

### Removing a friend

If you need to remove someone from your friend list:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the friend">
    Go to the **Amigos** tab and locate the friend you want to remove.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove friendship">
    Click the **Eliminar amigo** (Remove friend) button on their profile card.
    
    <Warning>
      Removing a friend means:
      - They can no longer see your public or friend-only entries
      - You can no longer see their public or friend-only entries
      - Any shared entries become invisible to both parties
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Friend tabs explained

The Friends page has three tabs:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Perfiles** (Profiles) | All MyDiary users except those who are already your friends |
| **Amigos** (Friends) | Users with whom you have an accepted friendship |
| **Solicitudes** (Requests) | Pending friend requests you've received from others |

## What happens when you're friends

Once a friendship is established:

- You can see each other's **public** entries in your feed
- You can be selected for **friends-only** entry sharing
- You appear in each other's friend lists
- You can share specific diary entries with them

<Tip>
  Build your friend network to make the most of MyDiary's sharing features. The more friends you have, the more options you'll have for selective sharing.
</Tip>
